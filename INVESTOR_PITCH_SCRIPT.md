### Recommended Approach:
1. **Record yourself** speaking 2-3 minutes of sample audio (for voice cloning)
2. Upload to **HeyGen** → it creates your avatar with your voice
3. Paste this script slide-by-slide
4. Add slide backgrounds as virtual presentation behind avatar
5. Export as MP4 → share via link or WhatsApp before meetings

### Tips for Maximum Impact:
- Keep avatar in **formal Indian attire** (blazer or kurta)
- Use **split screen**: you on left, slides on right
- Add **subtle background music** (corporate, low volume)
- Total video: **6-7 minutes max** — investors lose attention after that
- End with your **real phone number / email** for Q&A scheduling

### Backup: Record Yourself
- If avatar quality isn't satisfactory, record on phone with ring light
- Use **Canva** or **Google Slides** for slide backgrounds
- Edit in **CapCut** (free) — add captions automatically

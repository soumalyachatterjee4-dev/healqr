# THE MOAT (Why Competitors Can't Copy)

1. **SchoolHealth** locks families into HealQR from age 3
2. **Subdomain** gives doctors a digital identity tied to HealQR
3. **Medical Locker** stores 15+ years of health data — switching cost is infinite
4. **Advertiser network** grows with every new parent/patient
5. **Doctor is the distribution** — zero customer acquisition cost

```
Competitor would need to:
  ✗ Convince schools to switch (15 years of data locked in)
  ✗ Convince doctors to switch (website + patients + locker)
  ✗ Convince parents to switch (child's entire health history)
  ✗ Build advertiser network from scratch

This is practically impossible once HealQR has 1L doctors + 50L patients.
```

Trust & Ethics

Our Unbreakable Commitments

🚫

We Will Never Interfere with Medical Treatment

HealQR is a booking & management platform only. All medical decisions, prescriptions, and treatments are 100% under the doctor's authority. We will never suggest, override, or influence any medical decision under any circumstance.

🔐

We Will Never Sell Patient Data

Patient data is sacred. We will never sell, share, or monetize patient information to any third party. Doctor owns 100% of their patient data with full export capabilities.

👨‍⚕️

Doctor-First Medical Control

Assistants are empowered with dashboard access for operational efficiency, but full medical control — prescriptions, treatment plans, patient consultations — remains exclusively with the doctor.

📢

Only Non-Medical Advertising

Our sole revenue source is non-medical 3rd party advertising. We never promote medicines, treatments, or healthcare products. Ads are strictly for non-medical consumer brands only.

The Problem
Why India's OPD System is Broken
📋
Paper-Based Chaos
Most doctors use paper registers. No digital records. No patient history tracking. Prescriptions get lost. Handwriting is illegible.
📞
Phone-Call Bottleneck
Patients call the clinic, wait on hold, get busy signals. Receptionists juggle calls + walk-ins. Appointments get double-booked.
⏱️
Waiting Room Hell
Patients arrive early, wait 2-3 hours, have no idea when their turn is. No queue visibility. No cancellation notification.
�
App-Based Booking Trap
Existing booking apps require both doctor AND patient to download the app. Doctors pay monthly subscriptions. Patients abandon after one use. The platform owns the data — not the doctor.
Result: Wasted time, lost patients, doctor pays for someone else's platform, zero data ownership.

⚠️ Strategic Rationale & Operational Depth
  • Revenue Scaling: As HealQR reaches 1M+ daily bookings, the CPC/CPM value matures from ₹1.00/booking (standard) to ₹4.00/booking (premier ad placements), mirroring high-value communication platforms like WhatsApp (which currently charges ₹0.78 for simple notifications).
  • Optimized Touchpoints: Removed high-cost outgoing WhatsApp alerts in favor of integrated platform touchpoints including Consultation Complete & Review Requests and Digital Prescription Retrievals, maximizing margin per booking.
  • AI Strategy: Gemini 1.5 Flash provides the backbone for AI Rx Reader and AI Diet Charts, currently free for viral growth but monetized beyond 10 patients/month.
  • Infrastructure readiness: Costs include Geo-coding (Google), Multi-language services, and Firebase auto-scaling.
  • Zero-Barrier Entry: By keeping 100% of core features free for Doctors, Clinics, and Patients, we ensure hyper-adoption leading to a dominant market share by 2027.

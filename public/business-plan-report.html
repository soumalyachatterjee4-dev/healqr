⚠️ Important Considerations
  • Revenue Model: Based on guaranteed 8 ad impressions per patient. Optional views (reminder, follow-up) can increase revenue by 20-25%.
  • Current Infrastructure: Core platform already developed with Firebase Blaze plan, Cloudflare CDN (healqr.com), and analytics dashboard operational.
  • Infrastructure: Costs include Firebase, cloud hosting, CDN, database, monitoring, and team salaries/managed services.
  • Scalability: Architecture designed to handle 200K+ doctors with minimal changes required.
  • Risk Mitigation: Multi-region deployment ensures 99.9% uptime. Disaster recovery in place from Phase 3.
  • Technology Stack: Industry-standard tools (Firebase, Node.js, React, PostgreSQL) for easy handover to managed service providers.
  • Team Strategy: Build lean team Phases 1-3, then outsource to managed services in Phase 4 to reduce operational burden.
  • Partnership Structure: Revenue split accommodates corporate partnerships (e.g., 1 template reserved for partner branding).
  • AI Integration: Premium AI developer assistant (Claude 3.5 Sonnet) can reduce team size by 60-70% while maintaining code quality.

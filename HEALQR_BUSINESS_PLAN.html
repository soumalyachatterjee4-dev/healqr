We respect patient data privacy above all else. We will NEVER sell, share, or monetize any patient booking data, personal information, or doctor prescriptions to any third party — ever. Patient data is encrypted at rest in our database. Our revenue model is purely based on ethical 3rd party advertising on patient-facing pages.

🎯 Our Recommended Strategy
Phase 1 & 2 — PWA Only
May 2026 → Oct 2026
PWA covers 95% of required features. Zero additional cost. Instant deployments. Focus budget on user acquisition and API integrations. No app store dependency.
Phase 3 — Evaluate Native App
Nov 2026 → Jan 2027
At 60K users, if App Store presence becomes a business requirement (investor pressure, premium branding, biometric auth), initiate React Native build. Reuse 70% of existing React codebase. Estimated cost: ₹15-20L one-time + ₹3L/month maintenance.
Phase 4 — PWA + Native Hybrid
Feb 2027 → Mar 2027
Both PWA and Native App live. QR-based booking flow stays PWA (no download friction). Doctor/Patient dashboards get native app for premium experience. Best of both worlds.
💡 Key Insight: Our QR-based booking flow must remain PWA even with a native app — patients scan a QR, they shouldn't need to download an app first. The PWA loads instantly in any browser. Native app would be an optional premium add-on for frequent users (doctors & returning patients).
